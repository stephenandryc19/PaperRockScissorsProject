Paper/Rock/Scissors

Game Rules

The Rules of Paper/Rock/Scissors:
  • There are two players, both trying to defeat the other
  • Both players simultaneously choose to play rock, paper, or scissors
  • Your opponent in this game will be the infamous villain B(r)owser
  • If both players have the same choice, the game is a draw
  • If one player plays rock and the other plays scissors, rock wins
  • If one player plays scissors and the other plays paper, scissors wins
  • If one player plays paper and teh other plays rock, paper wins
For official Paper/Rock/Scissors competition, see the following:
  • World RPS Society
  • World RPS Association
